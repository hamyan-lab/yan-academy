Discover our mission to make Spanish learning accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of passionate Spanish educators, YANACADEMY was born from the belief that language learning should be accessible, engaging, and culturally immersive. Our name "YANA" comes from the Quechua word meaning "helper" or "assistant," reflecting our commitment to supporting students throughout their Spanish learning journey.

What started as a small online platform has grown into a comprehensive Spanish learning academy serving students from over 30 countries. We combine traditional teaching methods with modern technology to create an unparalleled learning experience.
